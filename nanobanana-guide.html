Nano Banana 2

Google's AI image model — lives right inside Figma

Why I use it

Most AI image generators feel disconnected from the design process — you generate something, download it, import it, resize it. Nano Banana 2 skips all of that. It runs as a Figma plugin, so AI-generated visuals go directly into your frames. It's powered by Google's Imagen model, which means unusually accurate text rendering and strong spatial reasoning — two things that actually matter for UI work.

What it's for

  • Generating placeholder images and hero visuals directly in Figma frames
  • Creating mockup-ready ad creatives and social banners at speed
  • Producing UI illustrations and custom icons without leaving your design file
  • Editing and extending existing images with AI (inpainting, background swap)
  • Generating multilingual marketing assets — text accuracy holds up in Japanese, German, Chinese

Cost

Free — limited daily generations
Pro — higher limits + 4K output

The free tier is genuinely useful for exploration. I upgrade when I'm doing a heavy visual production sprint and need high-res exports.

Get started

Install it as a Figma Community plugin — no separate account needed if you already have Figma.
