Claude

by Anthropic — the AI I reach for first

Why I use it

Claude is my go-to AI for design work because it gives thoughtful, nuanced responses without hallucinating wildly. It reads context really well, which matters when you're describing messy UX problems or asking for feedback on copy.

What it's for

  • Writing and refining UX copy — button labels, empty states, error messages
  • Synthesizing messy user research notes into clear themes
  • Generating multiple design directions to react to and pick from
  • Drafting design briefs, specs, and stakeholder updates
  • Talking through design decisions when you don't have a team to rubber-duck with

Cost

Free — Claude.ai (generous daily limit)
$20/mo — Pro (more usage + priority access)

The free tier is genuinely useful for everyday tasks. I upgraded to Pro when I started using it for bigger research synthesis sessions.

Get started

No download needed — it runs entirely in the browser.
